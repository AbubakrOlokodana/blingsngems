About Us

Our History

Welcome to Blings N' Gems Jewelry, the top internet-based Jewelry Manufacturer & wholesaler located in Kingston, Jamaica and established in 2021. Our unique high quality designs are competitively priced and proven to sell. We carefully select a wide variety of quality designs to appeal to diverse customer tastes from highclass wholesale jewelry to cheap wholesale costume jewelry lines. We take our best efforts to bring the market the latest jewelry design and classic jewelry styles with the strongest retail appeal. 100% customer satisfaction has been the most significant factor in our success.

Our Mission & Vision

The aim of Blings N' Gems is to beautify the world with our meticulously crafted fine jewelry. To inspire moments of happiness and celebrations. To enlighten and enrich lives. To embrace changes and strive for innovation. Lastly, we hope to break conventions, think out of box to enable us surprise our customers with our creativity.

Our vision is to bring to the world a portfolio of exquisitely designed jewelry brands that anticipates and satisfies people's needs and desires while still creating mutual and enduring value with customers and suppliers.
